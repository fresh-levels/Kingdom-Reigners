  • Vision
Kingdom Reigners Assembly Church is a vibrant and dynamic Christian community committed to spreading the love of God and the teachings of Jesus Christ. Nestled along the scenic Mombasa/Nairobi highway, our Mariakani branch provides a warm and welcoming space for individuals and families seeking spiritual growth and a meaningful connection with God.
At the core of our ministry is a commitment to biblically sound and practical teaching that equips individuals to live purpose-driven lives. Our seasoned pastors and ministers deliver dynamic sermons and engaging messages, rooted in the timeless wisdom of the Bible. Whether you are a seasoned believer or exploring faith for the first time, our teaching will inspire, challenge, and empower you to grow in your relationship with God and impact your world.
To preach the gospel of Christ to the outermost part of the world. Raising a standard of Hollyness in our generation and generations to come.
